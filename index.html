ABQ Wellness Strategy Game

Build the lab. Mix the formula. Upgrade your life.

Longevity Lab: ABQ Empire is an original game concept set in an Albuquerque-inspired desert world. Players build a secret wellness lab, create powerful longevity formulas, improve their status, and grow a health empire through smart choices.

Start the Mission

Game Concept

ABQ Desert Lab

Start with a small hidden lab in the desert and turn it into a high-tech wellness base.

Formula Mixing

Combine vitamins, herbs, minerals, and rare ingredients to create powerful wellness formulas.

Empire Growth

Sell products, earn money, unlock upgrades, and become a leader in the longevity boom.

Characters

The Lab Builder

The Lab Builder manages the lab, tracks money, plans upgrades, and keeps the wellness empire moving forward.

The Formula Maker, called "Cook"

Cook is the formula expert of the team. This character studies ingredients, mixes bold wellness formulas, and brings a secret desert-lab energy to the game without crossing into anything harmful.

The Fitness Coach

The Fitness Coach helps improve health, stamina, motivation, and reputation through training missions.

Gameplay Steps

Step 1: Collect Ingredients

Players collect vitamins, herbs, minerals, and special wellness items from different ABQ-inspired locations.

Step 2: Mix the Formula

Players use the lab to test combinations, create formulas, and discover stronger health upgrades.

Step 3: Grow the Empire

Players sell formulas, gain reputation, upgrade the lab, and expand their wellness business.
